# AI Quality Review

## Evaluation Summary

Tested all AI endpoints with multiple cloud security scenarios.

## Results

* /describe average score: 4.4/5
* /recommend average score: 4.2/5
* /generate-report average score: 4.5/5

## Observations

* AI responses were generally accurate and security-focused.
* Recommendations were relevant in most cases.
* Some responses lacked detailed remediation for advanced cloud scenarios.

## Improvements

* Added better prompt wording for remediation-focused outputs.
* Improved clarity for high-risk findings.
